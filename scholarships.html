Types of scholarships

If you are stuck, here is a list of potential scholarship types that you can look for that are more specific and focused. Sites like Fastweb and Unigo will have lists of scholarships for a specific identity such as the ones listed below.

University-specific

  • Department specific – Some colleges have department/program/major-specific scholarships within their school. For example, PLU has the STEM scholars one for STEM majors.
  • Visit scholarships! – Easy money, often times private universities will offer 500-1000 dollar scholarships if you visit their campus for a preview day or another event.
  • Merit – Some universities have separate merit scholarship applications while others do not. Check to see if your colleges do and plan ahead for those essays.
  • Need-based/grants – Same thing ^
  • Descendants of Alumni
