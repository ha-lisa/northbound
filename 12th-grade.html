NINTH GRADE TENTH GRADE ELEVENTH GRADE TWELFTH GRADE


Objective

You made it! The last grade in high school! This is YOUR year to make everything you have done up to this point count!




What you need to do

  • Continue taking academically challenging courses and MAINTAIN your grades!
  • Complete all the testing you need to do (SAT/ACT, SAT Subject Tests, AP, IB, etc.).
  • Decide on which colleges to apply for and finish those applications! You can often get fee waivers for those applications.
  • Lastly, have the TIME of your HIGH SCHOOL LIFE! It's senior season, so don't forget to maintain your friendships with your teachers and peers before you graduate and have less opportunities to see them.



Applying to College

Every college require different things. Some require letters of recommendations, SAT Subject Test scores, portfolios, interviews, etc. and some do not. Researching the colleges you want to attend is important.
